L'Oréal's 2022 Annual Report PDF Scraper

I developed this tool in Python to extract L'Oréal's Annual Sales Figures for 2022 from their PDF report and import them into an Excel file. Simply download their 2022 Annual Report, select the file from your device and click "Upload and Extract Sales". This is a proof of concept exercise for PDF scraping and exporting to Excel with future large-scale applications.
